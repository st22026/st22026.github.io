Nestled in the heart of mountainous splendor, our establishment offers a unique fusion of adventure and relaxation. Whether you're an avid mountaineer, a nature enthusiast, or someone seeking refuge from the hustle and bustle, our club and chalets provide the perfect escape.

Whether you seek the thrill of conquering summits, the serenity of untouched nature, or the warmth of a crackling fireplace, the Maunga Club & Chalets welcomes you with open arms. We invite you to be part of our community, to bask in the majesty of mountains, and to create cherished memories in the embrace of our chalets.

Club Membership

To become a member of Club Maunga, please follow the steps below:

  1. Email the following information to Club Maunga at maungaclub@maungaclub.maungaclub:
  2. Full Name
  3. Physical Address
  4. Proof of Address (e.g., copy of power bill)
  5. Proof of ID (Drivers Licence or Passport)
  6. Your membership will be confirmed via email.
  7. Additional information about the club and accommodation (membership letter) will be sent to you by regular mail.
